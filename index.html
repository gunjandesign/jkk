About Jungkook

Jeon Jeong-guk (전정국), better known by his stage name Jung Kook (정국), is a South Korean singer-songwriter and record producer under Big Hit Music. He is a vocalist, the center, a dancer, a visual and the maknae of BTS. As a solo artist, he has released first self-produced song "Still With You" in 2020.
